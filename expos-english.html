Open call for exhibitions

¡Sin letras!

Typography isn ́t only letters. There are alsopuntuation marks, meta typography... there is life beyond the letters! In Chulotype we believe that this aspect of typography can be very expresive, that ́s why we have an open call to encourage you to experiment with all those glyphs of a typography system that aren ́t letters. The result must be a poster with a message, the one you want.

Typo Gifs

In it ́s almost 30 years of history, the GIF format has evolve from an ancient system to lighten the image weight for an emerging World Wide Web. But where is type on the gif-world? That ́s why Chulotype encourages you to let your imagination run wild in order to spread moving type amongst a cyberwold full of pictures of kittens and 4chan memes. Let ́s flood the Internet with typo-gifs!

Cada letra cuenta una historia
From 11th October to 15th November


It means “every letter tells a story”, this exhibition that travels from the Valencia´s Muvim Museum, showcases the Spanish type design since 1990. Curated by Raquel Pelta and part of the Last Cit (Valencia Typography Congress) represents a chronological and conceptual tour with posters, type specimens, objets and video that will delight our audience!

Interactype
From 13th November to 26th November


Slowtrack will hold this exhibition where we can experience some european interactive projects that join together creativity, innovation and the most experimental part of typography. Also we will exhibit the two open calls driven by Chulotype: TypoGifs and ¡Sin letras! (without letters) Two different aproaches, animation and posters, where letters will come alive and will comunicate, with other codes, the typographic message.

Por todas partes. Graffiti & typography.
From 24th October to 29th November


We will see in this new gallery specialized in urban art, how the street signatures are made visible. The exhibition is based on video reports, interviews and process documentation which explores the relation between typography and street signatures. In addition we will have a round table discussion with the authors, where the dialog and the reflection will be the focus.
